Our Story

Artistry in Every Strand

Avenue Hair Studio is a salon nestled in the heart of Cyprus, dedicated to elevating your look through expert technique and personalised care. Our team of seasoned stylists blends modern trends with timeless craftsmanship.

We believe that a truly great haircut isn't just about the cut — it's an experience. From the moment you walk in, expect warmth, expertise, and results that exceed your expectations.
